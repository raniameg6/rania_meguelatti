About Me

A Voice Built
on Truth

I'm a Master's graduate in Mass Communication and New Media from the University of Setif 2, Algeria — where I earned high distinction for my research on American public diplomacy and its presence on Algerian television.

My journey in media spans radio broadcasting and print journalism. From hosting live radio segments at ASIA Radio to writing investigative feature stories at KAWALIS Newspaper, I bring the same commitment to every story: accuracy, depth, and human connection.

Based in Doha, Qatar, I am passionate about social issues, cross-cultural storytelling, and the evolving landscape of digital media and podcasting.
